I am proud of

Playing in a band

I've been playing the guitar and singing in a punk band for the last 5 years

It's been an adventure with a lot of ups and downs, (hello covid pandemic!) but I love it and wouldn't give it up for anything else

Playing music I like, travelling to new places, meeting new people... It makes me happy and proud!

Why I'm proud of it

  • I get to play music I like in front of people
  • It's my teenage dream come true (let's be honest)
  • I'm proud of the music skills I gained over the years along with my bandmates
  • I look cool on a stage!
  • I like overcoming the nervousness before a show and just playing through in spite of it
  • I kept on playing and putting energy into my band, even when it was harder or had a busy life
  • People enjoy it! Someome often comes to us at the end of a show to tell us they loved it

How I try to stay humble about it
